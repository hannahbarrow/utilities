Exploring Sewanee Water Use

by Hannah Barrow

2026-04-14

This data story is looking at Sewanee utility use, specifically water use in the buildings on Sewanee's campus. This data was collected by the university from 2023 to 2025. I found that the trends in water use are pretty uniform among different residential halls.
